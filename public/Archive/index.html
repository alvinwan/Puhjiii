Meet Our Talented Team

James Liu

James is an Entrepreneur who is dedicated to energy efficiency and making a positive impact on the community. He has over 10 years of experience in the environmental and energy industries and is well versed in project management, energy and water efficiency practices, smart meter monitoring, demand response generation, and renewable energy. He is the founder, CFO, and COO of getExp, specializing in custom big data systems, and is the co-founder and Chairman of Pinetree Energy Council, a 501(c)3 dedicated to improving energy and environmental efficiency for businesses and educating minority-owned businesses about the benefits of becoming more energy efficient. James earned his master's degrees in Electrical Engineering from UCLA and MBA from UC Davis.

Andrew Barkett

Andy Barkett has been a founder or co-founder of several successful businesses, including Greenlight Apparel and Getexp, Inc. and serves on the board of Free Ventures, the UC Berkeley student-run business accelerator. Andy has more than a decade of engineering and technology experience in a variety of fields - databases, search, semiconductors, and robotics. Andy led several infrastructure engineering teams at Facebook, where he was in charge of dozens of engineers on six production engineering teams responsible for thousands of servers and scaling systems in mobile infrastructure, messaging, advertisements, newsfeed, platform, and payments. Previously, Andy worked at Google, Livescribe, and NVIDIA. He recently served as the Chief Technology Officer of the Republican National Committee and currently serves as a Special Adviser to the Chairman. Andy earned a BA in Political Economy from the University of California at Berkeley and an MBA from the University of California at Davis.
